# Prerequisites

## OWallet

**OWallet** is a non-custodial wallet compatible with **EUENO** and required for the process of file storage, data encryption, and data sharing on **EUENO**.

**EUENO** will support a wider range of non-custodial wallets in the future.

**Here are steps to connect your wallet to EUENO:**

**Step 1:** There are options to connect your wallet:

- **Option 1:** Click on the **Connect Wallet** button on the top right corner of the page.
- **Option 2:** Click on the **Connect Wallet** button on the top right corner of the page.

![Blog theme](https://docs.eueno.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3701722239-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FQRsOb8nybzYU151xljhX%252Fuploads%252FIHncIwvIU56idQVMbXMf%252FScreen%2520Shot%25202022-11-25%2520at%252014.13.30.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D1ab8f0e7-7109-4ad7-b7f3-6797ca7ec625&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=2050fe53&sv=1)


**Step 2:** Click **Connect Wallet** at the top right corner of the page and **Approve** the permission request. You are good to go now.
