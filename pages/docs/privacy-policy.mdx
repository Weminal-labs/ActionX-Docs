## Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies Web Cookies

A “Web Cookie” is a string of information that a website stores on a user’s computer, and that the user’s browser provides to the website each time the user submits a query to EUENO. Like many sites, we use Web Cookies to track the pages that users visit during each browsing session, both to help us improve users’ experiences and to help us understand how EUENO is being used. 

As with other non-personal information gathered from users of EUENO, we analyze and disclose in aggregated form information gathered using Web Cookies, so as to help us, our partners and others better understand how EUENO is being used.

We may use third-party service providers who are authorized to place third-party cookies and may also set cookies on EUENO. In such cases, these third-party service providers are responsible for the cookies they set on EUENO. 

If you want further information, please go to the website of the relevant third party. If you would like to opt out of all other types of technologies we employ on EUENO, you may do so by changing your browser settings to block, delete or disable these technologies as your browser or device permits.
